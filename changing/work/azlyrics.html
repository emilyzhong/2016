AZ Lyrics Redesign
I worked in a group of three to create a modern redesign of the song lyrics website, AZ Lyrics, using HTML and CSS
The landing page features a carousel that compromises the background of the webpage, scrolling through featured album covers. This is paired with a search bar placed front and center, prompting users to either search for specific song lyrics or peruse the suggested featured ones.
The lyrics page places emphasis on the lyrics, keeping the background a simple dark gray. The upper and lower areas of the scroll include a gradient that create a modern and natural fade-in/fade-out effect of the lyrics, a thematic feature throughout the redesign.
The artists page contains a carousel that allows users to scroll through different albums of the artist. Each slide of the carousel displays the album cover along with the album's songs. With the list of songs, we use the gradient fade-in/fade-out motif to bring attention to the songs in the middle as the user scrolls.
