Logo Design

I've created logos as a part of production for the Daily Californian and as a member of Blue Team in Innovative Design. Below are a selected few.

Berkeley Beats

Berkeley Beats is part of the Daily Californian's multimedia projects, where local musicians are invited to the office to record live music, which is then released online. This is their logo that I created!

Title here.

Above anything else: having intent. Intent separates design from decor, and is just as important to have, as an artistic eye. What does this stroke weight imply? Is there a relationship between these separate lines of slab-serif text? If I can’t answer questions like these about something I placed on a page, it’s time to reconsider its necessity.
